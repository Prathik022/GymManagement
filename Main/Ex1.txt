Workout Plan for Active Individuals:
Day 1: Strength Training

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (jogging, cycling, jumping jacks)
Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Bent-over rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Lunges: 3 sets of 8-10 reps per leg
Plank: Hold for 30-60 seconds
Cool down: 5-10 minutes of stretching
Day 2: Cardio

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio
Running: 20-30 minutes at a moderate pace
Cycling: 20-30 minutes at a moderate pace
Jump rope: 15-20 minutes
Cool down: 5-10 minutes of stretching
Day 3: Active Recovery

Yoga or Pilates: 30-45 minutes of gentle stretching and relaxation
Walking: 30-45 minutes at a brisk pace
Foam rolling or self-massage: 10-15 minutes
Stretching: Focus on flexibility exercises for major muscle groups
Day 4: Interval Training

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio
High-intensity interval training (HIIT): Alternate between 30 seconds of high-intensity exercise (e.g., sprinting, burpees) and 30 seconds of rest for 15-20 minutes
Cool down: 5-10 minutes of stretching
Day 5: Strength Training

Same as Day 1 routine
Day 6: Cardio

Same as Day 2 routine
Day 7: Rest or Active Recovery

Rest day: Relax and allow your body to recover
Light activity: Gentle stretching, walking, or other low-impact activities
